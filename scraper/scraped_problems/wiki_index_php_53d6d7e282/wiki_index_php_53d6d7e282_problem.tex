\subsection*{Problem}
Let $N$ denote the number of ordered triples of positive integers $(a, b, c)$ such that $a, b, c \leq 3^6$ and $a^3 + b^3 + c^3$ is a multiple of $3^7$ . Find the remainder when $N$ is divided by $1000$ .
