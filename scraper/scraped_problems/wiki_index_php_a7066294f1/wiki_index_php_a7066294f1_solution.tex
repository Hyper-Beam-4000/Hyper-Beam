\section*{{2023 AMC 12A Problems/Problem 3}}
Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Solution}
Since $\left \lfloor{\sqrt{2023}}\right \rfloor = 44$ , there are $\left \lfloor{\frac{44}{5}}\right \rfloor = \boxed{\textbf{(A) 8}}$ perfect squares less than 2023 that are divisible by 5.
