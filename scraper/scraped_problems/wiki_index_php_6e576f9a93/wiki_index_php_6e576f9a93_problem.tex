\subsection*{Problem}
Define a sequence recursively by $f_1(x)=|x-1|$ and $f_n(x)=f_{n-1}(|x-n|)$ for integers $n>1$ . Find the least value of $n$ such that the sum of the zeros of $f_n$ exceeds $500,000$ .
