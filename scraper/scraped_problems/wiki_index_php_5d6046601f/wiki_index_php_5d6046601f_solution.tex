\subsection*{Solution}
Notice that $BC = AD = AP + PD = 3 + 2 = 5$ .
Now, because the question tells us $ABCD$ is a rhombus, $BC = AB = 5$ . Thus, by Pythagorean Theorem on $APB$ , $BP = 4$ .
