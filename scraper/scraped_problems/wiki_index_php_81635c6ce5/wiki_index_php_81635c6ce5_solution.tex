\subsection*{Solution}
Note that $n\equiv S(n)\bmod 9$ , so $S(n+1)-S(n)\equiv n+1-n = 1\bmod 9$ . So, since $S(n)=1274\equiv 5\bmod 9$ , we have that $S(n+1)\equiv 6\bmod 9$ . The only one of the answer choices $\equiv 6\bmod 9$ is $\boxed{(D)=\ 1239}$ .
