\subsection*{Problem}
Suppose that $a$ , $b$ , $c$ and $d$ are positive integers satisfying all of the following relations. \[abcd=2^6\cdot 3^9\cdot 5^7\] \[\text{lcm}(a,b)=2^3\cdot 3^2\cdot 5^3\] \[\text{lcm}(a,c)=2^3\cdot 3^3\cdot 5^3\] \[\text{lcm}(a,d)=2^3\cdot 3^3\cdot 5^3\] \[\text{lcm}(b,c)=2^1\cdot 3^3\cdot 5^2\] \[\text{lcm}(b,d)=2^2\cdot 3^3\cdot 5^2\] \[\text{lcm}(c,d)=2^2\cdot 3^3\cdot 5^2\] What is $\text{gcd}(a,b,c,d)$ ? $\textbf{(A)}~30\qquad\textbf{(B)}~45\qquad\textbf{(C)}~3\qquad\textbf{(D)}~15\qquad\textbf{(E)}~6$
