\section*{{2019 AMC 12A Problems/Problem 17}}
Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Solution}
Applying Newton's Sums , we have \[s_{k+1}+(-5)s_k+(8)s_{k-1}+(-13)s_{k-2}=0,\] so \[s_{k+1}=5s_k-8s_{k-1}+13s_{k-2},\] we get the answer as $5+(-8)+13=10$ .
