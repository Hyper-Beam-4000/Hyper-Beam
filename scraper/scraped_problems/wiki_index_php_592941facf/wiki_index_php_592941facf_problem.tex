Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Problem}
Find all polynomials $P$ with real coefficients such that \[\frac{P(x)}{yz}+\frac{P(y)}{zx}+\frac{P(z)}{xy}=P(x-y)+P(y-z)+P(z-x)\] holds for all nonzero real numbers $x,y,z$ satisfying $2xyz=x+y+z$ .
