\subsection*{Problem}
Let $S$ be the set of all integers $z > 1$ such that for all pairs of nonnegative integers $(x, y)$ with $x < y < z$ , the remainder when $2025x$ is divided by $z$ is less than the remainder when $2025y$ is divided by $z$ . What is the sum of the elements of $S$ ? $\textbf{(A)}~3041 \qquad \textbf{(B)}~3542 \qquad \textbf{(C)}~3750 \qquad \textbf{(D)}~4044 \qquad \textbf{(E)}~4319$
