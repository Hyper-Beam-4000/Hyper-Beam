Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Solution}
The mean and median are \[\frac{3m+4n+17}{6}=\frac{m+n+11}{2}=n,\] so $3m+17=2n$ and $m+11=n$ . Solving this gives $\left(m,n\right)=\left(5,16\right)$ for $m+n=\boxed{\textbf{(B)}~21}$ . (trumpeter)
