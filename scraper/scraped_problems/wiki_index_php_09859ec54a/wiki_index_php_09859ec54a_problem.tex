\subsection*{Problem}
Let $m$ and $n$ be positive integers satisfying the conditions $\quad\bullet\ \gcd(m+n,210)=1,$ $\quad\bullet\ m^m$ is a multiple of $n^n,$ and $\quad\bullet\ m$ is not a multiple of $n.$ Find the least possible value of $m+n.$
