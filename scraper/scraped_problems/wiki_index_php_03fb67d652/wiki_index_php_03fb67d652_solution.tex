\subsection*{Solution}
Since $a=1.5b$ , that means $b=\frac{a}{1.5}$ . We multiply by $3$ to get a $3b$ term, yielding $3b=2a$ , and $2a$ is $\boxed{\textbf{(D) }200\%}$ of $a$ .
