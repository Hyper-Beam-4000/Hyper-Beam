\subsection*{Problem}
Let $a > 1$ and $x > 1$ satisfy $\log_a(\log_a(\log_a 2) + \log_a 24 - 128) = 128$ and $\log_a(\log_a x) = 256$ . Find the remainder when $x$ is divided by $1000$ .
