\subsection*{Solution}
We have
\begin{align*}
\log_2x\cdot\log_3x&=2(\log_2x+\log_3x) \\
1&=\frac{2(\log_2x+\log_3x)}{\log_2x\cdot\log_3x} \\
1&=2(\frac{1}{\log_3x}+\frac{1}{\log_2x}) \\
1&=2(\log_x3+\log_x2) \\
\log_x6&=\frac{1}{2} \\
x^{\frac{1}{2}}&=6 \\
x&=36
\end{align*}
so $\boxed{\textbf{(C) }36}$ ~kafuu_chino
