Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Solution}
Each of the straight line segments have length $1$ and each of the slanted line segments have length $\sqrt{2}$ (this can be deducted using $45-45-90$ , pythag, trig, or just sense) There area a total of $13$ straight lines segments and $4$ slanted line segments. The sum is $\boxed{\textbf{(C) } 13 + 4\sqrt{2}}$ ~quacker88
