\subsection*{Problem}
Let $a,b,c$ be positive real numbers such that $a+b+c=4\sqrt[3]{abc}$ . Prove that \[2(ab+bc+ca)+4\min(a^2,b^2,c^2)\ge a^2+b^2+c^2.\]
