\subsection*{Solution}
We graph this on graph paper, with the scale of $\sqrt{2}:1$ . So, we can find $OT$ then divide by $\sqrt{2}$ to convert to our desired units, then square the result. With 5 minutes' worth of coordbashing, we finally arrive at $298$ .
