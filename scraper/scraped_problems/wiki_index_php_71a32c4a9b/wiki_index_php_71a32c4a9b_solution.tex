Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $x, y$ be our two numbers. Then $x+y = 4xy$ . Thus, $\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{y} = \frac{x+y}{xy} = 4$ . $\boxed{ \textbf{C}}$ .
