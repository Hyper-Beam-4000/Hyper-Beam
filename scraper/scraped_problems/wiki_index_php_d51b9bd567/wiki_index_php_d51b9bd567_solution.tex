\section*{{2025 AMC 12A Problems/Problem 19}}
Source: AoPS Wiki

\subsection*{Solution}
We begin by factoring:
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
&a^3b^2+a^2b^3+b^3c^2+b^2c^3+c^3a^2+c^2a^3 \\
&=a^2b^2(a+b)+b^2c^2(b+c)+c^2a^2(c+a)\\
&=(a^2b^2+b^2c^2+c^2a^2)(a+b+c) - a^2b^2c-b^2c^2a-c^2a^2b\\
&=(a^2b^2+b^2c^2+c^2a^2)(a+b+c) - (abc)(ab+bc+ca).\\
\end{split}
\end{equation}
From Vieta's Formulas, we know that $a+b+c = 0$ , $ab+bc+ca = k$ , and $abc = -1$ . Therefore, the answer equals $(a^2b^2+b^2c^2+c^2a^2)(0) - (-1)(k) = \boxed{k}.$ ~lprado
